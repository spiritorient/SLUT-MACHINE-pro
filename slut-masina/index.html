How to Play

  • Each spin costs 21 points.
  • Match 3 symbols to win a jackpot. Higher tiers pay more.
  • Match exactly 2 symbols to earn a small win and unlock a paid respin.
  • Every Trap deducts 5 points. Three Traps trigger the Wildcard bonus.
  • Wildcard acts as any symbol for matches and may trigger a special bonus.
  • Recharge appears automatically if your score drops below 50 (+300 points).
  • You can customize symbol art anytime from the Customize menu.
