How to Play

Spin

Each spin costs 21 pts. Reels can land on Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, Trap, or Wildcard.

Jackpot

3-of-a-kind (Wildcard substitutes): Bronze 60, Silver 100, Gold 160, Diamond 300.

Partial Win

2-of-a-kind (Wildcard substitutes): Bronze 15, Silver 20, Gold 25, Diamond 30. Unlocks Respin for 34 pts.

Why use Respin?

  • Aim to turn a partial win into a jackpot by rerolling the odd reel.
  • Best when your pair is higher tier (Gold/Diamond) or includes a Wildcard.
  • Up to 3 respins per round; if you run out, the Wildcard Bonus may trigger.

Trap

Each Trap deducts 5 pts. Three Traps trigger the Wildcard Bonus.

Wildcard

Acts as any symbol for matches. In the bonus, reels expand left-to-right; complete all three for +1000 pts.

Recharge

Appears if score drops below 50. Adds +300 pts.
